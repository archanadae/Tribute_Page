STEVE JOBS

( 1955 - 2011 )

  • 1955

    The Start.

    Steve was born on February 24th, and adopted into the Jobs family.

  • 1968

    The First Job.

    Is offered a summer job at Hewlett Packard (also known as HP).

  • 1972

    College.

    Jobs meets friend Steve Wozniak and attends Reed College in Oregon with him for 18 months.

  • 1976

    The Start of Apple.

    Steve Jobs and Steve Wozniak begin making Apple Computers

  • 1980

    The Millionaire.

    Became a millionaire at 25 years old.

  • 1984

    The Macintosh.

    Apple presents the Macintosh computer to the world.

  • 1985

    Disputes at Apple.

    The board at Apple forces Steve to loose all his power of the Apple business. He leaves Apple soon after.

  • 1997

    Returns.

    Steve returns to work at Apple

  • 2001

    The iPod.

    Steve Jobs and Apple introduce the iPod in 2001.

  • 2007

    The iPhone.

    Apple presented the iPhone to the world.

  • 2011

    The End.

“Being the richest man in the cemetery doesn't matter to me. Going to bed at night saying we've done something wonderful... that's what matters to me.”

-- Steve Jobs
